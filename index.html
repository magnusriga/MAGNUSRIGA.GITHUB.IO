ABOUT

ProffApps is an accelerator program designed to help early-stage companies develop their intial technology platform and close their first round of funding.

We engage rockstar coders, and work closely as one cohesive team to create a web application, mobile application, and/or web site you can be proud of.

Our model is built to attract and reward the web's strongest programmers.
Your product development is safe with us.

On the commercial side, ProffApps brings your company a team of experienced advisors with extensive business development experience and a network of early-stage investors. They are set to bring your company from an idea to an investable business.

ProffApps helps bridge the gap between technology and business.

Idea

Startups enter the program as early as idea-stage, some even before a single line of code has been written. Design and preparatory work begins.

MVP

Trusted top-tier developers are handpicked and assigned to each startup, uniquely qualified to help build a minimum viable product.

Customers

Startups connect with customers and partners. Feedback loops into the development phase for continued product tuning.

Fundraising

Throughout the program, ProffApps helps participants reach potential angel and seed investors. These contribute capital and open doors.
